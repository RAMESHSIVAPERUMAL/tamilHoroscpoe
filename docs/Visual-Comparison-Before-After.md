## Why Both Patterns Are Essential

### Horizontal + Vertical Lines (+ Pattern)
- Creates the **4x3 grid structure**
- Defines **top, middle, and bottom rows**
- Separates **left and right columns**

### Diagonal Lines (X Pattern)
- Divides **corner boxes** into proper sections
- Creates the **center triangular areas**
- Adds **traditional aesthetic**

### Together (+ and X)
- Forms **exactly 12 sections** for the 12 Rasis
- Creates the **authentic South Indian format**
- Matches **centuries of tradition**
