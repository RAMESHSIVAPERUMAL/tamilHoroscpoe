# Planetary Strength (Shadbala) - Simplified Guide

## What is Shadbala?

**Shadbala** (?????) means "Six Strengths" in Sanskrit. It's a method to calculate how **strong** or **weak** each planet is in a horoscope. A strong planet can deliver good results, while a weak planet may struggle.

Think of it like **planet fitness levels** - some are athletic, some are average, some need training!

---

## The Six Types of Strength

### 1. **Positional Strength** (Sthana Bala)
**What**: How comfortable is the planet in its current zodiac sign?

**Simple Rules**:
- ??? **Exalted** - Planet is super happy (like being at home)
- ?? **Own Sign** - Planet feels at home
- ? **Friend's Sign** - Planet is comfortable
- ? **Enemy's Sign** - Planet is uncomfortable
- ?? **Debilitated** - Planet is very weak

**Example**: Sun is exalted in Aries (strong) but debilitated in Libra (weak)

---

### 2. **Directional Strength** (Dig Bala)
**What**: Is the planet in the "right direction" in the chart?

**Simple Rules**:
- Sun & Mars ? Strong in 10th house (South/Midheaven)
- Moon & Venus ? Strong in 4th house (North/Nadir)
- Jupiter & Mercury ? Strong in 1st house (East/Ascendant)
- Saturn ? Strong in 7th house (West/Descendant)

**Think of it as**: Each planet has a "favorite corner" of the chart!

---

### 3. **Motional Strength** (Chesta Bala)
**What**: How fast is the planet moving?

**Simple Rules**:
- ?? **Retrograde** (moving backward) ? Maximum strength (60 points)
- ?? **Fast Direct motion** ? Good strength
- ?? **Slow Direct motion** ? Weak strength
- ?? **Sun & Moon** ? Always get average strength (30 points)

**Fun Fact**: Retrograde planets appear to move backwards - like doing moonwalk!

---

---

### 5. **Temporal Strength** (Kala Bala)
**What**: Strength based on **when** you were born

**Factors**:
- ?? **Day/Night**: Some planets are day-strong, others night-strong
  - Benefics (Jupiter, Venus, Mercury, Moon) ? Stronger during day
  - Malefics (Sun, Mars, Saturn) ? Stronger during night
  
- ?? **Moon Phase**: 
  - Benefics stronger during bright fortnight (New ? Full Moon)
  - Malefics stronger during dark fortnight (Full ? New Moon)
  
- ?? **Weekday Bonus**: Planet gets extra strength on its own day
  - Sunday ? Sun
  - Monday ? Moon
  - Tuesday ? Mars
  - Wednesday ? Mercury
  - Thursday ? Jupiter
  - Friday ? Venus
  - Saturday ? Saturn

---

### 6. **Aspectual Strength** (Drik Bala)
**What**: Which planets are "looking at" (aspecting) this planet?

**Simple Rules**:
- ?? **Benefic aspects** (from Jupiter, Venus, Mercury, Moon) ? Add strength
- ?? **Malefic aspects** (from Sun, Mars, Saturn) ? Reduce strength

**Special Aspects**:
- Jupiter aspects 5th, 7th, 9th houses from itself
- Saturn aspects 3rd, 7th, 10th houses from itself  
- Mars aspects 4th, 7th, 8th houses from itself
- Others aspect only 7th house (directly opposite)
